• [ ] square brackets are used to enclose sets or ranges
  • [ao]pe, will match the substring ape or ope
  • chap[3-6].txt will match chap3.txt, chap4.txt, chap5.txt and chap6.txt
  • ^ matches the pattern to the beginning of the line
  • $ matches the pattern to the end of the line
